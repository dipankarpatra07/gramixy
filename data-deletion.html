Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated:

If you want to delete your Gramixy account data (including saved posts and any connected Instagram authorization data), follow the steps below.

Option A: Email Request

  1. Email us at gramixy.support@gmail.com
  2. Subject: Delete my Gramixy data
  3. Include your account email used in Gramixy

We will delete your data and confirm by email.

Option B: Disconnect Instagram (when available)

When the “Disconnect Instagram” button is available in your dashboard, you can disconnect your Instagram authorization instantly.
